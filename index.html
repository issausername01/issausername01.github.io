About Me

Hello, I'm Blair Black! I'm passionate about archival work, community history, and storytelling through digital media. My experience spans media collections, music history, and cultural preservation.

Projects

Here are a few highlights of my work:

  • Project 1: Jack Vargas Papers - A collection documenting queer artist and writer Jack Vargas.
  • Project 2: Ivy Bottini Papers - A collection of Lesbian activist, Ivy Bottini's personal papers.
  • Project 3: Donovan Meher Collection - Digital music collection documenting rock music from approximately the 1980s to 2010s.
  • Project 4: Southern California California Punk Collection - Digital music covering punk music of primarily L.A. and Orange counties ranging from the 1970s through today.
